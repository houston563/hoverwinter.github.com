ex2.9 区间宽度

令 m:(a b) n:(c d) 表示两个区间：

m+n = (a+c b+d)

width(m+n) = (b+d - (a+c))/2 = (b-a)/2 + (d-c)/2 = width(m)+width(n)

m-n = (a-d b-c)

width(m-n) = (b-c - (a-d))/2 = (b-a)/2 - (d-c)/2 = width(m)-width(n)

m:(3 4) n:(1 2)

m/n = (1.5 4) width(m)/width(n) = 1 ≠ width(m/n) = 1.25

mn = (3 8) width(m)width(n) = 1 ≠ width(m*n) = 2.5
